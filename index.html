To use the visualization:
Click on two of those black dots to draw a line, lines have to go from a point to a point that is adjacent to it. Diagional lines are not allowed. When you are finished building your elaborate maze, press the Go button to run the visualization. To reset the simulation, Press Reset.
To go back to the last ran maze press the Back button.
Key: The Yellow lines represents the paths that the program checks. The green line represent that the program's final answer for shortest path from A to B
